Kitten Rescue

TinyKittens

TinyKittens, based in Fort Langley in British Colombia, rescues feral cat and kittens, and provides education about cat care. This rescue emphasizes compassion and goes above-and-beyond to provide the best of care to all cats, both domesticated and feral. They take in strays, manage feral colonies, assist other rescues, and work with a team of veterinarians to help as many cats as possible.

The non-profit rescue focuses on:

  • 24/7 YouTube livestream showing cat care, kitten hijinks and the reality of rescue - including live births
  • Feral colony management and Trap-Neuter-Return
  • Cat and kitten socialization
  • Rare medical cases and colony geneology
  • Photo catalog and links to Social Media pages for adopted graduates
